Creating chatbots to help users

This summer, two interns, with help from several colleagues from SIL Langauge Technology Use, have produced an astonishing number of chatbots that can help in over 29 languages!

Click on the speech icon on the bottom right corner to start the bot. It only shows in English but you can type your question in any language and it will switch and respond in that language.
